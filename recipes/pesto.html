Pesto Pasta

This is one of my absolute favorite dishes to make. This basil pesto pasta is super quick and easy to make, and only requires a few simple ingredients.

I could literally make and eat this every day and be happy, it is that good. It's great as a side dish to a filet of salmon, but it's more than worthy of being the main course at the dinner table.

Ingredients:

  • Basil
  • Pine nuts
  • Freshly grated parmesan
  • Garlic
  • Lemon Juice
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

Steps:

  1. Add pine nuts, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to food processor and combine
  2. Add basil and combine
  3. Add olive oil and parmesan cheese and combine
  4. That's it! Add to pasta and enjoy!
