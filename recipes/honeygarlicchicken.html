Honey Garlic Chicken

This recipe is so easy to make in a slow cooker.

Ingredients:

  • Honey
  • Garlic
  • Chicken
  • Soy sauce
  • Chili paste
  • Tomato paste

Steps:

  1. Mix together soy sauce, honey, garlic, chili paste, and tomato paste
  2. Add chicken to slow cooker
  3. Pour sauce over chicken, making sure chicken is submerged in sauce
  4. Set slow cooker on low for 5-6 hours, or high for 3-4 hours
  5. When finished cooking, serve with rice and vegetables and enjoy
